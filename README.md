# Chem-Review-6-7
Electronic Structure of Atoms and Periodic Properties

Disclaimer: This project does not aim to be a complete, in-depth review for the following topics, but is rather a refresher to easily teach the following concepts.


Covers the following content:

UNIT 6: ELECTRONIC STRUCTURE OF
ATOMS

a) The Wave Nature of Light
b) Quantized Energy and Photons
c) Line Spectra and the Bohr Atom
d) The Wave Behavior of Matter
e) Quantum Mechanics and Atomic Orbitals
f) Representations of Orbitals
g) Many-Electron Atoms
h) Electron Configurations and the Periodic Table


UNIT 7: PERIODIC PROPERTIES

a) Development of the Periodic Table
b) Effective Nuclear Charge
c) Sizes of Atoms and Ions
d) Ionization Energy
e) Electron Affinity
f) Electronegativity
g) Metals, NonMetals, and Metalloids
